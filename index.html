About the Program

Player development isn’t just about the work done inside the lines—it’s about preparing athletes for the moments that define them.

As a Student Assistant Coach for the Slippery Rock University NCAA D2 Women’s Basketball team, alongside my experience at the varsity high school and Hoop Group Scholastic League (HGSL) levels, my approach is rooted in collegiate-level preparation. We don't do stationary, fluff drills that don't translate to real competition. Every repetition in our training is engineered to push your skill limits, slow down the geometry of the floor, and elevate your situational basketball IQ.

But dominant on-court performance is only half the battle. Basketball is our vehicle to teach and learn critical life lessons. Through intense preparation, strict discipline, and mental toughness, we build the character required for athletes to win decisively in life, long after the final buzzer sounds.
